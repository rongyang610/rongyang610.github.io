About Me

Who Am I?

A Software Engineer.

My first taste of Software Engineering started in high school where I utilized my HTML and CSS skills to manage and update my school's NFL page. I then started college where I majored in Electrical Engineering. There I was exposed to other languages like: C++, PLC, and Assembly. This was where my passion for programming really grew, but I also enjoyed the aspects of being an Electrical Engineer. I graduated and landed my first job as an Inside Sales Engineer, where I implemented a company wide quoting spreadsheet that helped increase productivity. Afterwards, I started investing in cryptocurrency, where I started my own little business with a partner. I was programming the front-end of our application. After some ups and downs with the little start-up I decided to pursue Software Engineering at full speed. So I decided to attend a 12 week intensive bootcamp where I learned Ruby, Ruby on Rails, SQL, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, React, and Redux. I used what I learned to build a full stack project that uses Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, JavaScript, React, Redux, HTML, and CSS. I also then built a game that is built on HTML 5 Canvas, JavaScript, and CSS.

If you are interested in what I do, please feel free to contact me.
